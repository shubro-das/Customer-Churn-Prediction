# Phase 2 — Customer Intelligence & Business Analytics

## Phase Overview

Once a reliable analytical dataset has been established, the next objective is to transform raw data into meaningful business insights.

In this phase, the focus shifts from preparing data to understanding customer behavior, business performance, and operational trends. The goal is to answer real business questions that help stakeholders make informed decisions using data.

Rather than building predictive models, this phase emphasizes descriptive and diagnostic analytics through SQL and Python.

The insights generated during this phase will not only support business decision-making but also guide feature engineering and model development in the subsequent machine learning phase.

---

# Objectives

By the end of this phase, the project should have:

* A comprehensive understanding of customer behavior.
* Clearly defined business Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
* Data-driven insights that support business decisions.
* Customer segmentation based on behavioral and business characteristics.
* Analytical reports and dashboards for different stakeholders.
* A prioritized list of business factors associated with customer churn.

---

# Business Questions

This phase aims to answer questions such as:

### Customer Analysis

* Who are the bank's most valuable customers?
* Which customer segments generate the highest value?
* What characteristics define loyal customers?
* Which customers are at higher risk of churn?

### Product Analysis

* Which banking products have the highest adoption?
* Which products are associated with higher customer retention?
* Which combinations of products are most common?

### Financial Analysis

* Which customer groups contribute the highest revenue?
* How do balance, tenure, and product usage relate to customer value?
* Which customer segments are the most profitable?

### Behavioral Analysis

* How does customer engagement influence retention?
* What behavioral patterns are common among customers who churn?
* Which activities indicate strong customer loyalty?

### Geographic Analysis

* Which regions have the highest concentration of customers?
* Which regions experience higher churn rates?
* Are there regional differences in customer behavior?

### Operational Analysis

* Which KPIs should the marketing team monitor regularly?
* Which customer groups should retention campaigns prioritize?
* Which business metrics require continuous monitoring?

## KPI Development

* Customer Retention Rate

## Business Communication

# Why This Phase Matters

Machine learning should be driven by business understanding rather than curiosity alone.

By thoroughly analyzing customer behavior, product usage, and business performance before developing predictive models, the project ensures that future machine learning features are grounded in real business evidence.

This phase mirrors the work performed by data analysts and analytics engineers, whose insights help organizations understand customer behavior, prioritize business initiatives, and identify opportunities where machine learning can deliver measurable value.
