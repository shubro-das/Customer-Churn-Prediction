# Phase 2 Execution Roadmap — Customer Intelligence & Business Analytics

## Goal

Transform the analytical dataset into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.

The objective of this phase is to understand customer behavior, identify business opportunities, define measurable KPIs, and provide data-driven recommendations that improve customer retention and business performance.

The insights generated during this phase will guide feature engineering and machine learning development in the next phase.

---

# Milestone 1 — Business Questions & KPI Definition

## Objectives

Define the business questions and success metrics that will drive the analytical work.

### Tasks

* Identify key business questions.
* Define business KPIs.
* Identify stakeholder information needs.
* Prioritize analytical objectives.
* Document business assumptions.

### Deliverables

* Business question catalog
* KPI documentation
* Stakeholder analytics requirements

### Checkpoint

Before moving forward, verify that:

* Business questions are clearly defined.
* KPIs align with business objectives.
* Stakeholder requirements are documented.

---

# Milestone 2 — Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Objectives

Develop a comprehensive understanding of customer behavior, feature distributions, and relationships within the data.

### Tasks

* Perform univariate analysis.
* Perform bivariate analysis.
* Perform multivariate analysis.
* Explore customer demographics.
* Explore financial characteristics.
* Explore product usage.
* Explore customer engagement.
* Explore churn distribution.
* Identify important trends and anomalies.

### Deliverables

* EDA report
* Statistical summary
* Visualization library
* Initial business observations

### Checkpoint

Before moving forward, verify that:

* Major patterns have been identified.
* Data anomalies are documented.
* Key observations are supported by evidence.

---

# Milestone 3 — Customer Intelligence

## Objectives

Develop a deeper understanding of customer behavior and customer value.

### Tasks

* Identify valuable customer segments.
* Analyze customer loyalty.
* Analyze customer engagement.
* Analyze customer lifecycle.
* Compare churn across customer groups.
* Evaluate behavioral differences between churned and retained customers.

### Deliverables

* Customer intelligence report
* Customer segmentation summary
* Behavioral analysis report

### Checkpoint

Before moving forward, verify that:

* High-value customer groups are identified.
* Customer behavior patterns are documented.
* Churn characteristics are understood.

---

# Milestone 4 — Business Performance Analysis

## Objectives

Evaluate how business performance varies across products, customer groups, and operational metrics.

### Tasks

* Analyze product adoption.
* Analyze customer profitability indicators.
* Analyze regional performance.
* Analyze retention trends.
* Analyze operational KPIs.
* Identify business improvement opportunities.

### Deliverables

* Business performance report
* KPI analysis
* Operational insights

### Checkpoint

Before moving forward, verify that:

* Business performance metrics are calculated.
* Key performance drivers are identified.
* Opportunities for improvement are documented.

---

# Milestone 5 — Dashboard Development

## Objectives

Develop dashboards that communicate analytical findings to different stakeholders.

### Tasks

* Design executive dashboard.
* Design customer dashboard.
* Design operational dashboard.
* Build interactive visualizations.
* Validate dashboard accuracy.

### Deliverables

* Executive dashboard
* Customer intelligence dashboard
* KPI dashboard
* Dashboard documentation

### Checkpoint

Before moving forward, verify that:

* Dashboards accurately represent the data.
* KPIs are displayed correctly.
* Visualizations support business decisions.

---

# Milestone 6 — Business Recommendations

## Objectives

Translate analytical findings into actionable business recommendations.

### Tasks

* Summarize analytical findings.
* Recommend customer retention strategies.
* Recommend product improvement opportunities.
* Recommend marketing priorities.
* Identify important factors influencing churn.
* Document future analytical opportunities.

### Deliverables

* Executive summary
* Business recommendation report
* Customer retention recommendations
* Phase 2 review document

### Checkpoint

Before completing Phase 2, verify that:

* Every business question has been answered.
* Recommendations are supported by evidence.
* Insights are actionable.
* Findings provide clear direction for feature engineering and machine learning.

---

# Phase 2 Completion Criteria

Phase 2 is considered complete when:

* Business questions have been answered using data.
* KPIs are fully defined and reproducible.
* Customer behavior is well understood.
* Customer segments have been identified and documented.
* Dashboards effectively communicate business performance.
* Business recommendations are supported by analytical evidence.
* Key drivers of customer churn have been identified.
* The project is ready to begin feature engineering and machine learning development.
